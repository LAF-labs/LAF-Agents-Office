Ask each active role for:

- Yesterday: completed outcomes.
- Today: planned outcomes.
- Blockers: decision, tool, provider, integration, test, or memory.
- Checks: tests, review, deployment, or manual verification.
- Memory: Notebook drafts and promotion candidates.

CEO resolves priority. Reviewer flags risk. Ops reports provider, hooks,
deployment, and wiki sync health.
