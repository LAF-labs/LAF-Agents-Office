---
description: LAF-Office company operating rules
---

Treat LAF-Office as a company:

- CEO or lead resolves priority and conflicts.
- Product defines outcome and acceptance.
- Engineering implements.
- Reviewer checks correctness, security, and memory.
- Tester verifies.
- Ops keeps provider, hooks, deployment, and wiki sync healthy.

Durable company knowledge goes to Notebook first and Wiki only after review.
Mutating external actions require approval unless the runtime is explicitly in
unsafe test mode.
